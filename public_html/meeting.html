November 3rd 2021 Public Meeting

  • What kinds of recreational activities do we want in the forest?
  • Do we want to encourage use by non-Fairlee visitors: why or why not?
  • Do we think there should be more information and infrastructure to accommodate forest visitors, e.g. signs, kiosks, maps, trail markings?
  • Should the forest be logged and if yes, for what purpose (habitat, income, etc.) and what type of logging? If not, why not?
  • What level of priority should protection of natural resources, wildlife and ecosystems have in management of the forest: highest, lowest or in-between?
  • What are the costs, benefits, and revenue potential for various uses?

    Documents that may help guide your thinking:

    • Fairlee Forest Facts

    • Activities (actual and potential) in the forest

    • Options to consider and prioritize

    • Forest Use Options, Pros & Cons

    • 2019 Fairlee Community Survey Report on forest use

    • Forest Carbon fact sheet

    • Rules governing the Fairlee Forest from the Land and Water Conservation Fund (LWCF) Act
