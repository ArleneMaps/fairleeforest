Forest Governance and Administration Document Archive

Timeline of documents pertaining to the acquisition of the Lange Forest 1978-1980

Date            Description
July 23, 1978 The Langes' letter offering land
Feb 16, 1979 Craig Whipple (LWCF Grant Administrator)'s letter cautioning Selectboard Clerk Godfrey about Timber Sales
Feb 28, 1979Craig Whipple (LWCF Grant Administrator)'s letter cautioning Selectboard Chair Sargent about Timber Sales
Mar 5, 1979Craig Whipple (LWCF Grant Administrator)'s letter advising Selectboard Member Gatto about longterm responsibility
May 23, 1979 HCRS Final Approval and Agreement Includes Project Agreement and related forms “…a legal and binding contract stating that the Town will comply with all of the enclosed documents…"
Letter enclosed with the above agreement
Jan 28, 1980 Kee Committee expresses alarm at the Selectboard trying to rescind a 92-53 Town Vote
Mar 10, 1980 Agenda and minutes of the Selectboard meeting of 10 Mar 1980 adopting a resolution appointing a 5-member recreational board
Mar 15, 1980Letter from the Selectboard to the Langes offering to buy 770 acres from the Langes to avoid the grant conditions, without voter approval
Mar 17, 1980The Langes emphatically reject the Selectboard’s full-cash offer
Mar 17, 1980 The Selectboard reaffirms its motion of 10 Mar 1980, attached above, and even emphasizes the purpose of the appointed board’s purpose to manage the "FHCRS project"
Mar 17, 1980 The motion to create an appointed board is further moved to become a “proclamation” which will appear in the 1980 Annual Report under the heading of “RULES AND REGULATIONS OF FAIRLEE HERITAGE AND RECREATION SERVICE PROJECT (LANGE FOREST)”
Apr 23, 1980 The Selectboard moves to appoint the first 5 members of the newly formed board to oversee the grant terms and manage the project
